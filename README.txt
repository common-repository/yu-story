Yu Story is a WordPress plugin that lets you create and share interactive stories with ease, enhancing your site with engaging visual narratives.

== Description ==

Yu Story allows you to transform your WordPress website with an immersive stories. With an intuitive interface and powerful features, you can easily create and share interactive stories that captivate your audience. Engage your visitors with visually rich narratives, complete with custom URLs that lead to related content or external sites. Add descriptive text over your images to provide context. Whether you're a blogger, marketer, or educator, Yu Story provides the tools for share your stories.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'yu-story' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Use the 'Stories' post type to create new stories.
4. Add and manage your stories using the intuitive story editor.
5. Insert the '[yu_story]' shortcode into your posts or pages to display your stories.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Can I create multiple stories with this plugin? =

Yes, you can create as many stories as you want and display them on your website using shortcodes. Each story can have its own unique URL, allowing you to link directly to related content or external pages.

= Can I add URLs to my stories? =

Yes, Yu Story supports custom URLs for each story. You can link your stories to any page or post within your site, or to external websites, making it a versatile tool for directing traffic to specific destinations.

= Can I add text over the images in my stories? =

Absolutely! Yu Story allows you to overlay text on your images, giving you the ability to add captions, descriptions, or any textual content that complements your visual narrative.
